Core Platform

Engineered to Solve Real Community Challenges

We leverage modern geolocation, deduplication algorithms, and gamified mechanics to drive rapid community recovery.

Geotagged Reporting

Pin issues directly on a map using native GPS geolocation. Auto-fetch address markers to send pin-point coordinates to resolution units.

Smart Deduplication

Our algorithms perform a 50-meter radius collision check on active reports. Preventing duplicate noise and consolidating community reports.

Gamified Rewards

Earn reputation points and status badges on verified reports and successful fixes. Rise to the top of your neighborhood leaderboard!

Municipal Dashboard

Dedicated administration panel for city workers. Change report statuses, add notes, and trigger real-time notification updates back to citizens.

Real-Time Flow

How Issues Get Fixed

Watch how the platform forms a direct loop between citizens and local authorities in three simple steps.

01

Citizen Identifies

Snap a photo, pick the type of issue (pothole, leakage, etc.), and the system auto-pulls your precise GPS location.

02

Smart Verification

The system cross-references nearby issues to check for duplicates. New reports publish instantly and add points to your citizen level.

03

Authority Resolves

Municipal workers log into the Admin portal, claim the ticket, fix the issue, and mark it complete. You receive an instant dashboard update.
